Community guidelines
********************

* Contributions to :program:`PyLebedev` are always welcome and appreciated. Before doing
  so, please first read the `CONTRIBUTING <https://github.com/ifilot/pylebedev/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md>`_
  guide.
* For reporting issues or problems with the software, you are kindly invited to
  `to open a new issue on Gitlab <https://github.com/ifilot/pylebedev/issues>`_.
* If you wish to contact the developers, please send an e-mail to i.a.w.filot@tue.nl.
